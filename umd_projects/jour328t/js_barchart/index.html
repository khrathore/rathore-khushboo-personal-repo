Only Alice Munro was on the list four times

90% of the authors featured on the 'New York Times' best books list were only featured once. Only 8% were featured twice, 1.5% of them were featured three times and .5% were featured four times. Alice Munro was featured on the list in 1996, 1998, 2001 and 2004 for four fiction books she wrote. She won a Nobel Prize in 2013 and has written many short story collections. The authors featured three times were Philip Roth, Ron Chernow and Zadie Smith.
